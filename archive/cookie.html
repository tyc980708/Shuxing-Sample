About Cookie Usage on "A Drink, Please"

"A Drink, Please" utilizes cookie to keep track of a list of drinks appeared on your webpage, such that you will get one, and only one unique drink per day. Only the time of access of the page, and the list of drinks appeared will be recorded. Click here to display the list of cookies stored on your device.
If you don't want these information to be stored on your computer, you can disable them in the website settings. Keep in mind that this also means it is possible to get two drinks that are exactly the same on consecutive days.
